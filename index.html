About Us

Our Vision

To create an inclusive, transparent, and responsive governance system that leverages technology to empower every citizen and ensure equitable access to government services.

We envision a India where every citizen, regardless of their abilities or location, can participate fully in the democratic process and access government services with dignity and ease.

Our Mission

To transform governance through digital innovation, making government services accessible, efficient, and citizen-centric.

We are committed to breaking down barriers to access, ensuring that our digital platforms are inclusive, and continuously improving based on citizen feedback.

Organization Structure

The Government of India operates through a three-tier structure:

  • Central Government: Headed by the Prime Minister, with various ministries and departments
  • State Governments: Each state has its own government headed by a Chief Minister
  • Local Governments: Panchayats in rural areas and Municipalities in urban areas

This structure ensures governance at all levels, bringing services closer to citizens.

Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for government schemes online, follow these steps:

  1. Visit to official government portal
  2. Navigate to "Schemes & Services" section
  3. Select scheme you wish to apply for
  4. Register or login with your credentials
  5. Fill in to application form with required details
  6. Upload necessary documents
  7. Submit to application and note to reference number

Commonly required documents for government applications include:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID Card
  • Address Proof (Ration Card, Electricity Bill, etc.)
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport size photographs

Specific documents may vary depending on to scheme or service you're applying for.

To track your application status:

  1. Visit to "Track Applications" section in Citizen Corner
  2. Enter your application reference number
  3. Alternatively, login to your account and view your application history
  4. You can also check status through SMS updates if you've registered your mobile number
  5. For additional help, contact the helpline number provided during application

To file a grievance:

  1. Navigate to "Grievance Redressal" section in Citizen Corner
  2. Fill in the grievance form with details of your complaint
  3. Select the relevant department and category
  4. Provide supporting documents if available
  5. Submit the form and note to grievance registration number
  6. You can track the status of your grievance using the registration number

Accessibility

Screen Reader Support

This website is designed to be fully compatible with screen readers. We've implemented:

  • Proper heading structure (h1, h2, h3)
  • Descriptive alt text for all images
  • ARIA labels and roles for interactive elements
  • Skip links for quick navigation
  • Logical reading order

Keyboard Navigation

You can navigate this website using only your keyboard:

  • Use Tab to move between interactive elements
  • Use Shift+Tab to move backward
  • Use Enter or Space to activate buttons and links
  • Use Arrow keys to navigate within menus
  • Use Escape to close dropdowns and modals

Visual Adjustments

This website offers several visual adjustment options:

  • Text size adjustment (A-, A, A+ buttons)
  • High contrast mode toggle
  • Dark mode toggle
  • Focus indicators for all interactive elements
  • Consistent color scheme with sufficient contrast

Voice Reader

Use our built-in voice reader to have content read aloud:

  • Click the voice reader button in the top bar
  • Use Alt+R keyboard shortcut
  • Adjust reading speed and voice type
  • Pause, resume, or stop reading at any time
